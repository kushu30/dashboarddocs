# Create Buy X Get Y Discount

The **Buy X Get Y Discount** allows you to offer promotions where customers get additional items when they purchase a specified quantity or value. This is commonly used for “Buy 1 Get 1 Free” or “Buy 2 Get 1 at 50% Off” type offers.  

---

## Step 1: Setup

The **Setup** section determines how the discount is applied at checkout.  

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/buyxgety/image1.png"
  alt="Setup section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **Coupon Code:** Customers must enter the code at checkout.  
- **Automatic Discount:** Automatically applies when conditions are met.  
- **Discount Code Example:** e.g., `BUY2GET2`.  

---

## Step 2: Condition

The **Condition** section defines what customers must buy and what they will receive.  

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/buyxgety/image2.png"
  alt="Condition section"
/>

**Customer Buys:**  
- **Minimum Quantity:** Require customers to purchase a specific number of items.  
- **Minimum Purchase Amount:** Require a minimum spend.  
- **Apply To:**  
  - *Any items from the store*  
  - *Specific products*  
  - *Specific collections*  

**Customer Gets:**  
- **Quantity:** Number of items customers receive.  
- **Apply To:**  
  - *Any items from the store*  
  - *Specific products*  
  - *Specific collections*  
- **Discount Type:**  
  - *Free* – e.g., Buy 1 Get 1 Free.  
  - *Percentage off* – e.g., Buy 2 Get 1 at 50% off.  
  - *Amount off each* – e.g., ₹100 off selected items.  
- **Maximum Per Order:** Limit how many free/discounted items can be applied in a single order.  

---

## Step 3: User Eligibility

The **User Eligibility** section is identical to other discount types.  

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/buyxgety/image3.png"
  alt="User eligibility section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **User Specific:**  
  - *All users*  
  - *Specific users only*  
- **Order Specific:**  
  - *All orders*  
  - *First order only*  
  - *Based on total order count*  

---

## Step 4: Usage Limits

The **Usage Limits** section controls how many times the discount can be applied.  

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/buyxgety/image4.png"
  alt="Usage limits section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- Maximum total uses of the discount.  
- Maximum uses per customer.  
- Allow combining with other discounts.  
- Define the active start and end date/time.  
- Optionally disable Cash on Delivery (COD) when this discount is used.  

---

## Step 5: Preview

The **Preview** section helps customize how the discount is displayed to customers.  

<img
  className="rounded-lg"
  src="/images/coupons/buyxgety/image5.png"
  alt="Preview section"
/>

**Options include:**  
- **Coupon Visibility:** Show or hide discount coupon during checkout.  
- **Custom Title and Details:** Add descriptive text so customers understand the promotion clearly.  

---

## Why It Matters

Buy X Get Y discounts are effective for:  
- Increasing average cart size.  
- Moving inventory by promoting specific products or collections.  
- Driving repeat purchases with compelling offers.
