---
title: "Change Password"
---

Update account security by changing your login password.  

![Change Password Screenshot](/images/settings/image8.png)

## Key Actions
- Enter **old password** to authenticate.  
- Set a new strong password (min 8 characters, mix of letters, numbers, symbols).  
- Confirm the new password and save.  

⚠️ Best Practice: Update passwords periodically and never share them with others.
