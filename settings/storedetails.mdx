---
title: "Store Details"
---

The **Store Details** section allows you to manage your business information, credentials, and billing contacts.  
It is important to complete this setup so FlexyPe can authenticate your store and ensure compliance with business requirements.

![Store Details Screenshot](/images/settings/image.png)

## Key Actions
- **Store Credentials**: Verify `Store URL`, `Shopify Domain`, and ensure both **Admin API Token** and **Storefront API Token** are active.  
- **Business Details**: Fill in fields like Trade Name, Legal Name, GSTIN, and company information.  
- **Finance POC**: Add a billing contact for financial operations.  
- **Danger Zone**: Optionally disable FlexyPe checkout to revert to Shopify checkout.
