---
title: "Taxes & Duties"
---
Manage tax compliance for your store by configuring base tax rates and overrides.  

![Taxes & Duties Screenshot](/images/settings/image2.png)

## Key Actions
- **Regional Settings**: Review synchronized base tax values across states/regions.  
- **Tax Exemptions**: Add overrides for specific product categories or collections.  
- Ensure values align with your GST and federal tax obligations.
