The **Payments** section allows you to configure multiple payment gateways and link them to payment methods.  

![Payments Screenshot](/images/settings/image1.png)

## Key Actions
- **Choose Provider**: Configure and activate gateways like Razorpay, PhonePe, Cashfree, etc.  
- **Payment Methods**: Map UPI, EMI, Cards, Wallets, and Pay Later to the correct provider.  
- **Visibility**: Control which payment methods appear at checkout via Customization > Payments.
