---
title: "Users & Permissions"
---


Manage platform access for your team by adding users with appropriate roles.  

![Users & Permissions Screenshot](/images/settings/image6.png)

## Key Actions
- **Store Owner**: Cannot be reassigned; full control.  
- **Add User**: Provide email and assign access level.  
- **Permissions**: Control what each user can access.  
- Remove inactive users when no longer needed.
