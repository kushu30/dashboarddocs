The **Integrations** section allows you to connect FlexyPe with external platforms for analytics and marketing automation.  

![Integrations Screenshot](/images/settings/image3.png)

## Key Actions
- **Ads & Analytics**: Enable integrations like **Meta Pixels, Google Analytics, and Google Ads**.  
- **Marketing Automation**: Add tools such as 11za or Abandoned Cart Webhooks for improved conversion.  
- Track performance across campaigns and automate re-targeting.
